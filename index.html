How to use an awesome reading tool...

Assembly time: 10 minutes
Awesome rating: 70% awesome

Are you Neurodivergent or have difficulty reading? Much of the population does. 20% of the population is affected by dyslexia and over 4% of adults are affected by ADD/ADHD. I am going to teach you how to use a tool called Bionic Reading that helps make reading easier for neurodivergent folks. It works by helping peopleâ€™s eyes and attention track through the text.

  • Things You'll Need

  • Computer or Smartphone
  • Internet Access
  • A Willingness to Learn!

  1. Open internet browser and type in https://bionic-reading.com/.

    I used Google but you can use any browser.

  2. Click on Bionic Reading Web app icon.

    The web App button is the third button down. Feel free to click through the other buttons to explore the interface and other apps.

  3. A new page will open and will display a text editor.

    You can type in or paste text here and then click the process button and it will turn Bionic.

  4. You can also upload documents or turn entire webpages to the Bionic Font.

    Click through the three different buttons at the top of the browser to explore.

  5. There is also a web browser extension for Bionic Reading!

    Enjoy and happy reading! I hope this font helps you read a little smoother and please tell your friends and teachers about it. It's a real life saver! :)
